How to use this gamepad tester

  1. Connect your controller via USB or Bluetooth
  2. Press any button to activate browser detection
  3. Check buttons, analog sticks, triggers and vibration
  4. Use drift status to evaluate controller health

Supported controllers

Works with Xbox controllers, PS4 / PS5 controllers, Nintendo Switch Pro controllers and most standard USB or Bluetooth gamepads.

What is this Gamepad Tester?

This online gamepad tester lets you test controller buttons, analog sticks, triggers and vibration directly in your browser without installing software.

FAQ

Why is my controller not detected?

Make sure your controller is connected and press any button to trigger browser detection.

Does this support PS5 controllers?

Yes, most Chromium-based browsers support PS5 DualSense controllers.

Why does vibration not work?

Vibration depends on browser and system-level support, so it may not work everywhere.
