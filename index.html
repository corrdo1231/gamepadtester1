How to use this gamepad tester

  1. Connect your controller with USB or Bluetooth.
  2. Press any button to let the browser detect the device.
  3. Check the highlighted buttons and analog stick movement.
  4. Test triggers and see whether vibration is available in your browser.

This tool is designed for quick controller checks directly in the browser. It is useful for diagnosing button issues, analog stick drift, trigger pressure and mapping problems.

Supported controllers

This online controller tester works with many common devices, including Xbox controllers, PlayStation controllers, Nintendo Switch Pro controllers and generic USB gamepads.

Best experience is usually on desktop Chrome or Edge. Some features, especially vibration, may vary depending on browser, operating system and controller support.

FAQ

Why is my controller not detected?

Make sure the controller is connected properly and press any button. Some browsers require user interaction before the gamepad becomes visible.

Does this work with PS5 and Xbox controllers?

Yes. In most desktop browsers, Xbox and PlayStation controllers can be detected and tested, including buttons, sticks and triggers.

Why does vibration not work?

Vibration support depends on browser, operating system and controller compatibility. Some browsers do not fully support vibration testing.

How can I check stick drift?

Release the analog stick and watch the X/Y values. If the stick is not centered when untouched, your controller may have minor or significant drift.
