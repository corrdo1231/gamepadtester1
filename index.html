How to use the tester

The flow is simple, but these three steps give you the cleanest read on drift, deadzone, and button health.

1. Connect and wake the controller

Plug in with USB or pair with Bluetooth, then press any face button once. Some browsers only expose a pad after the first interaction.

2. Release both sticks

For drift checks, keep your thumbs off the sticks and watch the live magnitude and peak idle offset numbers for a few seconds.

3. Sweep every input

Press A/B/X/Y, LB/RB, Start, Select, D-pad directions, and then roll both sticks through full circles to confirm travel and trigger range.

FAQ

Short answers for the most common controller testing questions.

Do I need to install anything?

No. GamepadTester.tools is a plain static website that reads controller input directly from the browser.

Why is my controller not showing up?

Browsers often require a button press after connection. If it still does not appear, reconnect it and try a current Chrome, Edge, or Firefox build.

What does the suggested deadzone mean?

It is a lightweight estimate based on the highest idle stick offset observed after your last reset, plus a small safety margin.

Can this test Xbox and PS5 controllers?

Yes. Standard-mapped Xbox pads and PS5 DualSense controllers usually work well when connected through a supported desktop browser.
