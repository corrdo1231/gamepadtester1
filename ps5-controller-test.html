PS5 controller checklist

These quick checks cover the inputs most likely to matter in shooters, racing games, and remote play tools.

Face button response

Press Cross, Circle, Square, and Triangle in quick succession and confirm the active state updates without delay.

Trigger travel

Pull L2 and R2 slowly to see whether their values rise evenly. Sudden jumps can suggest a hardware or mapping issue.

Center return

After rotating both sticks to the outer edge, release them and verify the visualization returns near the center point cleanly.
