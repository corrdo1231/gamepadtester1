Privacy Policy

How this site handles analytics and browser-based controller data.

This page explains the basic privacy practices for GamepadTester.tools. It is intended as a straightforward summary for users visiting the site and using the browser-based controller tests.

Short version

The site uses Google Analytics 4 for general traffic measurement and reads controller input locally in the browser to power on-screen diagnostics.

Information collected automatically

Like many websites, GamepadTester.tools may collect basic analytics and technical usage information such as page views, device type, browser information, approximate region, referrer data, and interaction events.

Google Analytics 4

The site uses Google Analytics 4 with the measurement ID G-E1950FMW07. Google may process analytics data in accordance with its own policies, cookies, and retention settings.

Controller input handling

Controller state is read through the browser Gamepad API to display live results on the page. The site is designed as a static front end and does not intentionally upload raw controller input streams to a custom backend.

Cookies and similar technologies

Analytics providers may use cookies or similar identifiers to help measure traffic, session behavior, and aggregate site performance.

Third-party links and tools

If the site links to third-party resources, those services operate under their own privacy practices. Users should review the relevant third-party terms and privacy notices when appropriate.

Policy updates

This privacy policy may be revised over time as the site changes. Continued use of the site after updates means the latest posted version applies.
